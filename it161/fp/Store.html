We established ourselves in 2007 setting up shop in the Central District neighborhood not far from the Seattle World School, located near 18th and Union Streets. We have had some different iterations over the years, but finally settled in the Yesler Terrace neighborhood in 2019.

Our mission is to offer up the best coffee and the experience associated with either your drop-in or online shopping. When you visit our location in the Yesler Terrace neighborhood (located at Yesler and Broadway), the baristas will treat you like family and invite you in. Here the neighborhood coffee shop is alive and well. On top of our award-winning Linea organic coffee options, we offer an excellent assortment of baked goods and eats from locally established bakeries and restaurants. But most importantly, we welcome people into our space to enjoy the camaraderie of connectivity and friendship associated with great coffee and eats.
