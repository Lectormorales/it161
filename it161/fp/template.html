Who are we? At Tougo Coffee Co., Coffee is our priority, but community is our passion. You cannot have a good coffee experience without being in a great coffee house or coffee shop. There was a time when coffee houses were known for camaraderie, lively debates, and furtive discussions. Public events and gatherings were the norm. Artists and writers used to enter these denizens of intellectual growth to expand their creative minds.

At Tougo Coffee Co., we embrace this philosophy and welcome people in as they are. We welcome different groups and ask all to have an enlightened approach to each other. We are all humans on this marvelous blue and green marble called Earth.
